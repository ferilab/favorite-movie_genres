###             Analysis of users' rating on Netflix movies                ###


1- pre-processing.R reads the data from dslab and saves it in rdas subdirectry. Then explores the data
to find which years' movies have received sufficient number of ratings. The movies (based on their years of first screening) with too low number of ratings are removed from the data. 
2- analysis.R further anylizes the data to see:
  - if most frequently rated movies are of the highest rating;
  - what are the most favorable genres in users views?

The plots are saved in fig directory.
